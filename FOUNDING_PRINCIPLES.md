# Burch Platform - Founding Principles

1. Customer First
Every feature must improve the customer experience.

2. AI by Default
Every workflow should consider how AI can assist.

3. Mobile First
Most users will access Burch from their phones.

4. Partner Success
If hotels, restaurants, and organizers succeed, Burch succeeds.

5. Simplicity
Build simple solutions before adding complexity.

6. Security
Protect customer and partner data.

7. Scale
Design for Africa, build for the world.
